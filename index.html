How to Play

Don't know how to play? Don't worry, here is how.

Snake

Press up, down, left or right to move.

Apple

This you need to catch as much as you can.

Wall

Don't hit them, otherwise you're dead.

Bee

Don't eat, otherwise you're dead too.

You can change the point of view by pressing the mouse button, this will give you a view from the top. Click again and you're back to the original. In snake reloaded if you move the mouse, the camera moves too, in both views, just to complicate things a bit :)
